✨ About Us – OvenBerries

At OvenBerries, we don’t just serve food — we curate experiences.

Our story began with The Cottage Coffee, the first destination in Nanded to introduce a refined, specialty coffee culture. What started as a passion for exceptional brews has gracefully evolved into a sophisticated, fusion-style café and restaurant — where global flavors meet artisanal craftsmanship.

☕ Specialty Coffee

Meticulously brewed for the perfect balance of aroma and flavor.

🍕 Woodfired Pizza

Crafted with authenticity and finesse in our traditional oven.

🍝 Continental Cuisine

Thoughtfully curated for a modern palate with global influences.

🥐 In-house Bakehouse

Delivering freshly baked, artisanal delights every morning.

Designed to offer both comfort and charm, our space features refined indoor air-conditioned seating alongside a beautifully curated outdoor setting, allowing guests to choose their perfect ambience.

Every detail at OvenBerries — from the plating to the lighting, from the first sip to the last bite — is intentionally crafted to elevate your experience.

This is more than a café.

This is where taste meets sophistication, and moments turn into memories.

OvenBerries — an experience, redefined.
